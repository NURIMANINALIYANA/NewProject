MY EDUCATION 🎓

In this page, I will share about my education. As the picture is state above, currently now I am further my study in Diploma of Information Management at UiTM Negeri Sembilan branch, Campus Rembau. This course is actually have 5 semester around 2 years and a half, besides, now I am in my last semester which is semester 5. At first, I didn't know about the existence of Information Management even when I applied this course to further my study after SPM , I still don't know what is this course about. I applied my diploma course with my mother and she suggested this course to me. After the result come out, I got this course and I love it.


When I was young, I started my education since age 4 at Langkawi, Kedah. My first kindergarten was Nuri Kindergarten and Perpaduan Kindergarten when I was age 5 and 6. SK Seri Negeri, Langkawi was my first primary school from 7 years old then I moved to SK Jitra II until I was 8 years old. My family used to moved here and there because of my father's occupation. So, I have many different school to continue my study. Next, I moved to Johor and continue my study since 8 years old until 14 years old at SK Kompleks Sultan Abu Bakar, SMK Kompleks Sultan Abu Bakar and SMK Medini which is my first hostel school that I attended. Even though I have many different school in my life, I am so glad that I met very nice people around me. Moreover, at age 15 and early 16, I moved to Kedah and my third secondary school was SMK Bandar Baru Darulaman. Then, I am interested to further my study at technical school and I applied it. Alhamdulillah, I got an offer after attending the interview session at SM Teknik Tuanku Jaafar or known as TJTI at Seremban, Negeri Sembilan. I finished my school journey at TJTI and I am so happy of having those experience of education at many places that I go. Prepared by: Nur Imanina Liyana binti Haini, 2019214302, 5D.
